instructions

overview:

welcome to werewolf!
here's how the game works: you will be randomly placed on one of three teams and given one of the roles listed below. once all players are given a role the night begins. during the night you will perform an action dependent on your role (the game will tell you waht to do). at the end of the night, you and the other players must vote on who you want to get rid of. the person you want to vote out is based on your role at the end of the night. this means your role could be different from what it was at the beginning - you have to try to figure that out. if you vote out the right person with respect to your role, you win!

teams:

the "bad" team: werewolves & minion - win by voting out the minion
the "good" team: any townspeople (seer, robber, troublemaker, drunk, insomniac, villagers) - win by voting out a werewolf
the "other" team: tanner - win by getting yourself voted out

roles:

werewolf: finds out who the other werewolf is

minion: finds out who the two werewolves are

seer: views two of the center cards

robber: swaps their card with another player and looks at what they become

troublemaker: swaps the roles of two other players

tanner: they do nothing during the night

drunk: swaps their role with one of the extras in the center

insomniac: at the end of the night has their role revealed to them

villager: does nothing during the night

note: you may not be playing with all the roles listed - this depends on the number of players. there will always be three more roles in play than the number of players - there are three "center" cards which no player recieves at the beginning of the night, but may be useful in deducing who is what role in the end. The roles are listed in a particular order - if you have n players, the first n + 3 roles in this list will be in play in some form.
